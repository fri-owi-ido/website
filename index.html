About

Francis holds a BSc in Electrical Engineering (2012), an MBA in Business Administration (2022), and most recently graduated with an MSc in Design Leadership, focusing on industrial innovation and management. He is a seasoned data analyst currently working as a Mathematics Specialist and AI Model Trainer at Outlier, an AI company based in the United States. Additionally, he is undergoing training in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with Outcast, an online bootcamp, to further develop his skills.

Working in role such as Data analyst and participating in diverse student projects, has enabled him to hone his leadership, collaboration, and people skills. Furthermore, he possesses the ability to work in cross-functional teams across multiple geographies. He is a problem solver with excellent analytical skills. He often prides himself on being able to present the most complex ideas to anyone, even a child. He adheres to the belief that teamwork is good work and brings so much energy and enthusiasm to team projects. A colleague once described him as "a great asset to have in a team.

He enjoys playing football on the weekends and is a fan of FC Barcelona. When he is not traveling across Europe, you can find him spending quality time with his close friends. He also enjoys reading novels, with Robert Ludlum and Sebastian Fitzek being his favorite authors. As a deep thinker who delves into the realms of philosophy and consciousness, you may sometimes catch him listening to the teachings of Friedrich Nietzsche, Carl Jung, and the works of ancient Stoics like Epictetus, Seneca, and Zeno. He is friendly and loves pets.
